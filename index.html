Why this exists

Most exam preparation is broken

Scattered notes, conflicting advice, and low-quality courses waste the most valuable thing you have: time. This platform replaces that with one clear, reliable system.

The usual experience

  • Preparation scattered across random sources
  • Overwhelming volume with no priority
  • Misinformation and unreliable shortcuts
  • No roadmap, no way to measure progress

The Ballpoint approach

  • Structure — a clear sequence from basics to mastery
  • Clarity — concepts explained, not just memorised
  • Direction — know exactly what to study next
  • Results — built around real exam outcomes
